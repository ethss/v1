How to Get an IMDb ID:

To find the IMDb ID of a movie or TV show:

  1. Go to the IMDb website (imdb.com).
  2. Search for the movie or TV show you want.
  3. On the search results page, click on the title of the movie or TV show to open its page.
  4. In the URL of the page, you will find the IMDb ID after the "title" parameter. It typically starts with "tt". For example, in the URL "https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0111161/", the IMDb ID is "tt0111161".
